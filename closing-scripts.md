## RESPONSE #4 - HANDLING OBJECTIONS

### "Can I make changes?"

Absolutely! That's the whole point of the sample. Tell me what you'd like different:
- Different colors or style?
- Add/remove sections?
- Change photos or text?
- Adjust layout?

I'm happy to make revisions before you decide.

---

### "I need to think about it"

Totally understand - it's a big decision.

A few things to consider:
- The sample stays live for 7 days at no charge
- $50/month covers everything - no hidden fees
- You can cancel anytime if it's not working

Take your time, and let me know when you're ready. Happy to answer any questions in the meantime.

---

### "Can you lower the price?"

I appreciate you asking! Here's the honest answer:

$50/month covers:
- Professional hosting (usually $10-20/month alone)
- Your custom domain (usually $15/year)
- Unlimited support and updates
- SSL security certificate
- Regular backups

For what's included, this is already well below market rate. Most web designers charge $500+ upfront and $100+/month.

That said - if you commit to 6 months, I can offer the first month free. That's $250 for 6 months instead of $300.

Would that help?

---

### "What if I cancel?"

No problem at all. Here's how it works:
- You can cancel anytime - no contracts
- I'll give you 30 days to export anything you want
- You keep your domain after 3 months
- No cancellation fees, no penalties

You're not locked into anything.
