FAQ

Answers for Daycare Owners

Here’s everything you need to know before booking your Consolto call.

Here to help

How long does the project take?

We deliver within three business days. Day 0 is the recorded intake call, Day 1-2 is building and revisions, and Day 3 is launch.

Can I request changes?

Yes, each build includes one round of minor edits (text tweaks, color adjustments, image swaps). Additional revisions require an add-on fee.

Do I own the website?

Absolutely. You receive WordPress/Oxygen logins plus a quick guide for contacting us. We hold admin access to protect the build but give you editor access for updates.

What’s the payment process?

Pay in full, split 50/50, or leave a $99 deposit via UniBee, PayPal Pay Later, CashApp, or Zelle.

What happens after a year?

Renew hosting, domain, and support for $149/year. We’ll remind you 60 days before renewal.

Can you help with Google Business Profile?

Yes, it’s an optional add-on. We prepare the listing, upload photos, and walk you through verification.

What do you need from me?

Just the Consolto call. Upload any logos/photos, tell us your preferred colors, and we handle everything else.

Still have questions?

Reach out anytime or head back to the services and pricing pages for more detail.
