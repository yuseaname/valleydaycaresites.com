## 6. QUICK TIPS FOR OUTREACH

### Cold Email Best Practices:
1. **Personalize** - Mention their daycare name, something specific about their business
2. **Keep it short** - Under 150 words
3. **One CTA** - Just ask them to reply
4. **Send Tuesday-Thursday** - Best response rates
5. **Send 9-11am** - Their local time

### Flyer Distribution:
- Childcare licensing offices
- Daycare supply stores
- Community centers
- Coffee shops near daycares
- Direct mail to licensed daycares

### Phone Call Best Times:
- 10-11am (after morning drop-off)
- 1-2pm (nap time)
- Avoid: 8-9am (drop-off) and 4-5pm (pick-up)
